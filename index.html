Projects

Project Title: DaaCure – Hospital Management System

Description: The DaaCure project is a smart, all-in-one hospital management system designed to make healthcare operations smoother, improve patient care, and ensure resources are used wisely. It brings together everything a hospital needs into one easy-to-use platform, helping staff focus on what matters most—caring for patients.

Key Features:

  • Patient Admission Optimization: Assign hospital beds and rooms to patients based on their condition and care requirements.
  • Doctor Scheduling: Assign doctors to patients or departments based on specialization and workload to minimize waiting times.
  • Emergency Room Response: Calculate the fastest routes for ambulances and emergency personnel, factoring in traffic and distance.
  • Resource Allocation: Efficiently distribute medical supplies based on demand and usage patterns.
